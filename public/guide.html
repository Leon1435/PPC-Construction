CONSTRUCTION GUIDE

Your comprehensive guide to planning and executing a successful renovation project

Planning Your Renovation

  • • Define clear goals and priorities for your space
  • • Set a realistic budget with 15-20% contingency
  • • Gather inspiration and create a vision board
  • • Consider timeline and temporary living arrangements
  • • Research local permits and building regulations

Design Considerations

  • • Think about long-term functionality and lifestyle needs
  • • Maximize natural light and optimize space flow
  • • Choose timeless materials that won't date quickly
  • • Plan for sufficient and strategic storage solutions
  • • Balance aesthetic appeal with practical functionality

Working with Contractors

  • • Request multiple quotes and check references
  • • Verify proper licensing, insurance, and credentials
  • • Review all contracts and warranties thoroughly
  • • Establish clear communication protocols
  • • Document all decisions and changes in writing

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • • Underestimating budget and timeline requirements
  • • Rushing the planning and design phase
  • • Prioritizing trends over timeless design
  • • Overlooking proper ventilation and lighting
  • • Compromising on quality to save costs

Typical Renovation Timeline

2-4 WEEKS

Planning & Design

Initial consultation, design concepts, and finalization

1-3 WEEKS

Permits & Approvals

Obtaining necessary permits and regulatory approvals

1-2 WEEKS

Demolition & Preparation

Site preparation and removal of existing structures

6-12 WEEKS

Construction

Main construction work including structural, electrical, and plumbing

2-4 WEEKS

Finishing & Installation

Installing fixtures, painting, and final touches

1 WEEK

Final Inspection

Quality check and handover
